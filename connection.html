CONNEXION

A connection game is a type of abstract strategy game in which players attempt to complete a specific type of connection with their pieces. This could involve forming a path between two or more endpoints, completing a closed loop, or connecting all of one's pieces so they are adjacent to each other. Event has 2 rounds and the team who correctly finds the clue and finds the answer is the winner.



    RULES

  • A team can consist of a maximum of 2 members.
  • Event consists of 2 rounds.
  • Round 1 - This round is based on quizes related to connection. Top 10 teams of this round are shortlisted to round 2.
  • Round 2- This Round is Completely based on Connections.
  • Winners are decided based on numbers of correct connections made by the team and also based on time.
  • Limited seats only. First come, first serve basis.
