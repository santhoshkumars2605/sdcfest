TECH QUIZ

Speed!! Speed!! Speed!! Battle of Brains is a Technical Quiz game of questions and answers on all topics of TECHNICAL knowledge that is played by technical students. The game is typically played with timing based on some number of teams, most commonly teams of two players each. A moderator reads questions to the teams, whose players endeavor to answer in first with the correct answer, scoring points for their team



    RULES

    First Round

  • A quiz will be conducted on the Website. The link will be provided on Spot.
  • The first round consists of 15 questions. (Objective question)
  • 2 marks for the correct answer, and no negative marking for the wrong answer
  • The answering time is only 30 seconds will be allocated.
  • Selected teams will be moving to the 2nd round.

    • Second Round

  • The second round consists of 10 questions. (objective questions)
  • 5 marks for the correct answer, no negative marking for the wrong answer
  • The answering time is only 20 seconds will be allocated.
  • Selected teams will be moving to the 3rd round
